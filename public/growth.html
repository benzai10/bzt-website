Current Routines (Habit Loops)

Morning Routine

  • Get up between 4:30 and 6:00 AM (I usually set a timer for 6 hours instead of setting an alarm)
  • Take a shower or wash face
  • Make the dishes from the evening before and clean the kitchen
  • Make a cup of coffee
  • Spend 1 to 2 hours with reading, learning, preparing for the day
  • Spend at least 10 minutes practicing something related to music
  • Work, get in contact with people etc.
